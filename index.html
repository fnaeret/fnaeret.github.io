Project Overview

Five Nights at Epstein's was a viral meme game that was quickly scrubbed from the internet. We are archiving it properly by reverse-engineering the logic, ripping the original assets, and rewriting the core mechanics.

This ensures the game remains playable, moddable, and preserved without relying on the original, bloated executable.

Technical Goals

  • Engine Replacement Moving from Unity to a lightweight, custom C++/SDL2 engine.
  • Cross-Platform Native support for Windows, Linux, macOS, and WebAssembly.
  • Asset Preservation Extracting and documenting all models, textures, and audio.
